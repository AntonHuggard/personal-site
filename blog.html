12 RULES FOR WEB DESIGN

April 2022

Most websites are terrible. They make a bad first impression and are then poorly-designed, slow or just annoying. There are very few websites that are genuinely pleasant to use. Obviously, if you want to attract traffic to your site, you should not be user-hostile. Here are 12 rules to keep in mind.

1. DO NOT USE COOKIES.

Very few first-time users care about your site. They have no intention of coming back. They want no relationship with your site. The average user probably doesn't even fully understand what cookies are - so asking for them probably makes them slightly confused and suspicious. Cookies are usually unnecessary and definitely not worth the negative response asking is likely to provoke. Don't ask for cookies.
